AAA Thai Language School Vocab Dictionary

What this is:

It's a super simple Thai/English Dictionary that contains the vocabulary from our classes (levels 1-3). Definitions are searchable using either English, or the unmarked phonetic equivelent of the Thai word (what it sounds like). Search results show all entries in text books levels 1-3 with the day, and level listed below. This is the first step towards a comprehensive dictionary I am developing.
